About

Gal Gadot is a 36 year old Israeli Actress. Born on 30th April, 1985 in Petah Tikva, Israel, she is famous for Wonder Woman/Diana Prince in Batman vs Superman: Dawn of Justice. Her zodiac sign is Taurus.

Gal Gadot has been in 5 on-screen matchups, including Chris Pine in Wonder Woman 1984 (2020), Jon Hamm in Keeping Up with the Joneses (2016), Sung Kang in Furious 6 (2013), Tom Cruise in Knight and Day (2010) and Oshri Cohen in Kicking Out Shoshana (2014).

Gal Gadot Varsano (Hebrew: גל גדות‎ born 30 April 1985) is an Israeli actress, producer, and model. At age 18, she was crowned Miss Israel 2004. She then served two years in the Israel Defense Forces as a soldier, after which she began studying at the IDC Herzliya college, while building her modelling and acting careers.
